Who We Are

Kings Bakes – Born from a Love of Baking

Kings Bakes was founded with one simple dream: to bring the warmth of a home kitchen into every product we sell. Located in the heart of Sundar Nagar, Tiruchirappalli, we have been serving freshly baked goods to students, families, and event organisers since 2019.

From rich birthday cakes to golden crispy puffs, everything at Kings Bakes is made fresh daily with no preservatives. We take pride in knowing our customers by name and making sure every bite brings a smile.
